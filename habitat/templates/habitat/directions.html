Directions to
Cloof Wine Estate

Start in the direction of the N1 highway northbound. Follow the signs leading to Paarl.

From the N1 merge onto the N7 highway towards Malmesbury. Continue driving on N7 for approximately 50 kilometers until you get to Malmesbury.

Turn left on the R315 towards Darling. Continue driving until you get to the train tracks, then turn left towards Darling Cellars.

Carry on straight on the dirt road for about 5km until you get to Cloof Wine Estate, on your right hand side.

VERY IMPORTANT
Do not turn left toward Chatsworth or Kalkbaskraal as Google Maps suggests.
